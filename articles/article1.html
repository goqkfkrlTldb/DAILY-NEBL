The Birth of Nebl City

Humanity has finally constructed a fully automated city. Today marked the inauguration of this groundbreaking urban achievement. The ceremony was attended by the mayor and various officials, while prospective citizens looked on with anticipation for this new beginning. The mayor announced that the city would be named "Nebel City," derived from the word "Nebel," meaning "fog." The name reflects the resolve to navigate the current uncertain times successfully.Nebel City is entirely self-sufficient, severing all external contact while operating and producing resources solely through its automated systems. Humanity will no longer suffer from pollution or the burden of labor, with safety and stability now assured.
